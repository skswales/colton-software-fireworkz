Typing text

You type text into Fireworkz just as you would into any word processor.

When you reach the right hand margin the caret will automatically move to the left margin and begin a new line. If a word you were typing at the end of one line proved to be too long to fit, it is automatically carried over to the next line. This is known as word wrap or auto wrap.
