Templates

You can save yourself a lot of time and effort by saving your page designs as templates. If you do this, you will be able to create new documents based on them without having to go through all the procedures listed in the sections above. Instead, the page details will be predefined and styles will already be set up. Template documents may also include text, such as fax headers or letterheads, and this is another way to save time.

For examples of stationery and template files, look at the directory Templates on the Examples disc. This includes several useful templates, for documents such as memos, faxes, and letters.

Other templates are included with Fireworkz, in the directory !Fireworkz.Resource.Template (RISC OS) or %USERPROFILE%\Colton Software\Fireworkz\Template (Windows).
