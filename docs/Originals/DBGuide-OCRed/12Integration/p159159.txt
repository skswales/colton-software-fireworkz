Fireworkz Pro 159

Integration within
Fireworkz Pro
Fireworkz Pro combines the word processing abilities of Wordz,
the sheet abilities of Resultz and the database facilities provided
by Recordz. The outcome is a powerful integrated package. This
chapter looks at some of the ways in which the word processing
and spreadsheet areas of the system can make use of databases. It
also covers some of the other improvements added to Fireworkz in
addition to the database related features.
It includes sections on:
. Database formats.
. Database functions.
. Mail merging.
. Non-database improvements.
