Database Guide 195

Date formats

Date fields can accept dates in a number of different formats.
These are listed below, together With an example of each. The key
is as follows:
d Day of month as a 1 or 2 digit number.
m Month as a 1 or 2 digit number.
y Year as a 1 or 2 digit number to which 1900 will be added.
Y Year in full.
a Optional AD/BC.
D Name of day (1st, 2nd ... 31st).
M Name of month either in full (January...December) or just the
first three letters (Jan...Dec).
