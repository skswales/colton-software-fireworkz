Bold

Bold text is used for emphasis, and to make areas of text stand out from the page. It is also used to provide weightier-looking type for headlines.

To embolden text:

  1. Select the area of text you wish to embolden.
  2. Click on the Bold button on the toolbar.
  3. The selected text will appear in boldface.

To enter text directly in bold, click on the Bold button while no text is selected. When you have finished with bold, click on the button again to deselect it.
