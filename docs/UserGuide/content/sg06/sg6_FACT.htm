FACT

Calculates the factorial[1] of a number.

Syntax:

FACT(n:number)

Returns the factorial of n (most commonly shortened to n!).

n must be an integer greater than or equal to zero.

Formula:

n! = ∏i=n i

i.e.

n! = n × (n − 1) ... × 2 × 1
