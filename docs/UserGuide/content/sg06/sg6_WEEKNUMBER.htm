WEEKNUMBER

Returns the week number corresponding to a date value.

Syntax:

WEEKNUMBER(date)

The week number corresponding to date is returned in numeric form.

On RISC OS the current territory is used to determine what the week number is.

On Windows, the first week of the year is considered to be the first whole week in a year, with a week starting on a Monday.

Revisions:

This function was added in 1.30/01.
