F.DIST.RT

F-distribution[1] - right-tailed probability.

Syntax:

F.DIST.RT( x:Number, degrees of freedom in the numerator:PositiveInteger, degrees of freedom in the denominator:PositiveInteger )

Returns the right-tailed probability of the F-distribution with given degrees of freedom in the numerator and degrees of freedom in the denominator at x.

Revisions:

This function was added in Fireworkz 2.00.
