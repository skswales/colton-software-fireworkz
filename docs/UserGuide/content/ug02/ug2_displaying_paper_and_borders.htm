Displaying paper and borders

Use the Display section of the View control dialogue box to choose which area is displayed in the window.

  • Full displays the full area including printable area, paper and gap between pages.
  • Printable area displays the printable area of the page including headers and footers, but not the paper margins.
  • Work area displays the main body of the page but does not include headers and footers.

Click on the required button and then on the OK button. The view will immediately change in the document.
